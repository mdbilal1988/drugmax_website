About DrugMax - Executive Summary

Founded in 2013 and based in Bangalore, DrugMax Private Limited is a global leader in the Export and Import of high-purity Botanical Extracts and Essential Oils. We specialize in bringing pharmaceutical-grade precision to the botanical B2B marketplace, ensuring standardized potency and supply chain transparency for global nutraceutical partners.

Our Mission

To empower the backbone of agriculture — our farmers — through direct sourcing and fair trade. We eliminate intermediaries, ensuring that those who grow our ingredients receive the value they deserve while we deliver absolute purity to our clients.

Our Vision

To define the global standard for sustainable health raw materials. We envision a world where clinical precision meets organic integrity, providing wellness that is completely traceable and fluidly delivered from soil to shelf.
