Rosemary Extract: The Natural Solution for Clean Labels

Why Carnosic Acid and Rosmarinic Acid are replacing synthetic antioxidants in the global food and beauty sectors.

As consumer demand for "Clean Label" products reaches an all-time high in 2026, manufacturers are rapidly phasing out synthetic antioxidants like BHA and BHT. The most effective natural alternative? **Rosemary Extract.**

The Power of Carnosic Acid

Unlike many plant extracts that are purely flavor-driven, Rosemary extract contains specific polyphenols—primarily **Carnosic Acid** and **Rosmarinic Acid**—that possess extraordinary antioxidant properties.

  • Lipid Stability: Carnosic acid is oil-soluble, making it ideal for preventing rancidity in oils, fats, and meat products.
  • Shelf-Life Extension: Natural Rosemary extracts can match or exceed the performance of synthetic preservatives in stabilizing sensitive cosmetic formulations.
  • Flavor Neutrality: Advanced extraction techniques allow for the removal of the characteristic rosemary aroma, providing a purely functional, neutral ingredient.

Standardization for Performance

At DrugMax, we provide Rosemary extracts standardized to precise levels of Carnosic Acid (up to 25%). This ensures that our B2B partners can calculate exact dosages for their specific preservation needs, maintaining consistency across global production lines.

"Sustainability in 2026 isn't just about the source—it's about the utility. Our Rosemary extracts provide a functional, plant-based solution that answers both the consumer's need for transparency and the manufacturer's need for stability."

B2B Applications

From high-performance skincare to the stabilization of pet foods and edible oils, standardized Rosemary extract is becoming a non-negotiable ingredient. Its heat stability makes it particularly valuable for baked goods and processed functional foods.
